# Ex Cinere — Survivors, Labor & Governance Systems

In Ex Cinere, every human life is finite capital. The simulation centers on the management of survivors and the political structures they form.

---

# 1. SURVIVOR SYSTEM

Each player manages a group of survivors. Survivors are named characters with specific roles and are the core assets of your caravan.

## 1.1 Survivor Roles

| Role | Primary Function | Combat/Economic Buff |
|---|---|---|
| Scout | Proximity Detection | Increases BLE scanning efficiency; early raid warning. |
| Medic | Healing & Recovery | Reduces recovery time for injured survivors after raids. |
| Engineer | Camp & Maintenance | Speeds up camp upgrades and reduces resource decay. |
| Cook | Resource Efficiency | Reduces Food/Water consumption rates. |
| Guard | Defense & Security | Increases combat power during raid resolution. |

---

# 2. HARDCORE PROGRESSION & DEATH

CARAVAN operates a strict hardcore model.

## 2.1 The Level System (1–99)
- Players progress from Level 1 to 99 through survival, trading, and successful raids.
- Higher levels unlock access to better gear tiers and higher political/syndicate thresholds.

## 2.2 Hardcore Death
When a player's last survivor is killed:
- **Level Reset**: Player level resets to 1.
- **Loot Drop**: All equipped items and held resources drop as a BLE loot chest.
- **Persistence**: Reputation, Infamy, and Camp upgrades are preserved.
- **Memorialization**: Fallen survivors are recorded in the Lost Caravan log.

---

# 3. REPUTATION & INFAMY

Social standing persists through death and defines a player's role in the wasteland.

| Metric | Source | Threshold Unlocks |
|---|---|---|
| Reputation | Successful governance, fair trading, chain building. | Political candidacy, nation leadership. |
| Infamy | Raiding, black market trading, syndicate activity. | Syndicate leadership, Raider mode perks. |

---

# 4. GOVERNANCE & NATION SYSTEMS

Players can form and lead nations, which are opt-in political allegiances.

## 4.1 National Leadership
Elected leaders gain control over:
- **Monetary Policy**: Setting the backing for the nation's Valuables.
- **Trade Policy**: Implementing tariffs and embargos.
- **Treasury**: Managing national resource reserves and Valuables issuance.

## 4.2 Political Destabilization
- **Currency Collapse**: If a nation's Valuables exchange rate crashes, a snap election is triggered.
- **Reputation Loss**: Attacking players or poor management causes reputation decay, leading to disqualification from office.

---

# 5. SYNDICATE SYSTEMS

Syndicates are shadow power structures centered on Infamy and the control of Cipher.

## 5.1 Syndicate Powers
- **Cipher Minting**: Issuing syndicate-specific scrip.
- **Bounties**: Placing Cipher rewards for the death of specific players.
- **Protection Rackets**: Offering safety to Merchants in exchange for tribute.

---

# DESIGN INTENT

The survivor system makes every loss personal, while the hardcore progression ensures that high-level players remain vulnerable. Politics and syndicates provide the macro-stakes that drive the street-level survival game.
