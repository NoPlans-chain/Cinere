# Ex Cinere — National Economic Archetypes

In the GPS-less world of Ex Cinere, Nations are defined by their economic strategy and monetary policy rather than physical geography. Any player group with sufficient Reputation can form a Nation and adopt one of these archetypes.

---

# 1. THE CORE (Industrial/Tech Sovereignty)
**Archetype: Value-Added Manufacturing & Innovation**
- **Focus**: Transforming raw scrap into high-tier equipment and survivor gear.
- **Monetary Policy**: Typically commodity-pegged to Scrap or Energy, ensuring stable production costs.
- **Primary Logic**: High efficiency multipliers but high maintenance costs for member camps.
- **National Bonus**: "Technological Leap" - Small chance for camp upgrades to provide double the storage/production capacity.

---

# 2. THE PERIPHERY (Resource Extraction Sovereignty)
**Archetype: Scale-Based Export**
- **Focus**: Large-scale extraction of Food, Water, and Fuel.
- **Monetary Policy**: Often free-floating or pegged to a basket of survival resources.
- **Primary Logic**: High volume, low margins. Minimal infrastructure requirements for member camps.
- **National Bonus**: "Scavenger's Luck" - Increased yield when scavengers/raiders bring resources into national territory/proximity.

---

# 3. THE ARCHIPELAGO (Trade & Logistics Sovereignty)
**Archetype: Mercantilism & Transit Taxation**
- **Focus**: Moving goods between industrial and extraction-focused players.
- **Monetary Policy**: Mixed reserve, holding a basket of other nations' Valuables and Cipher.
- **Primary Logic**: Revenue generated per transaction/redemption rather than production.
- **National Bonus**: "Merchant's Protection" - Reduced raid risk and faster bond renewal for members in Merchant mode.

---

# 4. MONETARY POLICY & BACKING

National leaders must declare a backing method for their Valuables.

| Backing Method | Description | Risk |
|---|---|---|
| Commodity-pegged | Redeemable for a fixed resource (e.g., 1 Valuables = 10 Water). | Vulnerable to resource embargos. |
| Free-floating | Value determined by market supply/demand. | Susceptible to bank runs and coordinated sell pressure. |
| Mixed Reserve | Nation holds a basket of resources and Cipher. | Requires active treasury management by the leader. |

---

# 5. CURRENCY DESTABILIZATION

Nations can be targeted for economic collapse by coordinated syndicate actions.
- **Cipher Flood**: Syndicates dumping Valuables reserves to crash the exchange rate.
- **Embargo Inflation**: Cutting off the peg commodity to drive hyperinflation.
- **Snap Election**: A currency collapse triggers an immediate removal of the current leader.

---

# DESIGN INTENT

These archetypes create a natural division of labor in the wasteland. By choosing an archetype, a Nation defines its role in the global order book and its relationship with the syndicates, ensuring that political choices have immediate economic consequences.
