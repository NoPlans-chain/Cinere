# Ex Cinere — Information Asymmetry & Intelligence Access

In Ex Cinere, information is a resource class. Proximity reveals immediate threats, but strategic intelligence reveals macroeconomic shifts.

---

# 1. INFORMATION ASYMMETRY

The world is not perceived identically by all players. Visibility into the simulation depends on your Intelligence Access Tier, which is unlocked through Reputation, Infamy, and the specific composition of your survivors (Scouts).

---

# 2. INTELLIGENCE ACCESS TIERS

| Tier | Name | Typical Roles | Information Access |
|---|---|---|---|
| T0 | Civilian | New players, low-level | Proximity events only; market ticker. |
| T1 | Commercial | Traders, Merchants | National resource reserves; early market trend signals. |
| T2 | Institutional | Corporate leaders | Global order book depth; commodity supply projections. |
| T3 | Political | Nation leaders, advisors | Intelligence on rival nation treasuries and embargo plans. |
| T4 | Strategic | Intelligence heads | Syndicate Cipher flow; hidden raider band deployments. |
| T5 | Inner Circle | Syndicate heads, high-infamy | Predictive world-state data; high-priority bounty targets. |

---

# 3. THE SCOUT ROLE

Scouts are the primary source of intelligence for a caravan.
- **Proximity Scanning**: High-level Scouts increase the BLE detection range (~10m -> ~15m) and provide early warning of Raider signals.
- **Strategic Recon**: Scouts assigned to "Recon" missions provide T1-T2 intelligence data regardless of player reputation.
- **Raid Intel**: During the 2-minute raid resolution window, Scouts may reveal the opponent's survivor allocation or gear usage.

---

# 4. MARKET FORESIGHT

Players with high Intelligence Access receive "Early Signals" before they manifest in the global ticker.
- **Supply Shocks**: Awareness of a looming commodity shortage 5-10 ticks before the price spikes.
- **Political Instability**: Early warning of a currency collapse or snap election in a rival nation.
- **Cipher Volatility**: Signals indicating a syndicate is about to flood the market with Valuables.

---

# 5. RISK OF KNOWLEDGE

High intelligence access increases a player's profile in the wasteland.
- **Assassination Risk**: High-tier players (T4-T5) are automatically broadcast as "High Value Targets" in syndicate bounty networks.
- **Stress & Decay**: Maintaining high intelligence tiers increases the stress load on survivors, slightly accelerating resource consumption and injury risk.

---

# DESIGN INTENT

Information asymmetry ensures that power is not just about wealth or combat, but about the ability to predict and manipulate the simulation. This creates a "hidden" layer of gameplay where intelligence gathering is as critical as resource scavenging.
